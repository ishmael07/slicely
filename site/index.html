How it works

From a sentence to print-ready G-code, in one conversation.

💬

You ask

Tell the chat what you want. It’s powered by Claude — pick Opus, Sonnet, or Haiku in the model menu.

🔎

It finds (or you upload)

Searches Thingiverse, Printables & MakerWorld — or drag in your own STL · 3MF · OBJ · STEP or a ZIP of parts.

🧠

It reasons

Reads the model’s real geometry, then dials in layer height, infill, supports & brim for your goal.

🍰

It slices

Hands it to PrusaSlicer and generates clean G-code — with real print time, filament & cost.

🖨️

You print

Open the result in PrusaSlicer or save the G-code straight to your printer’s SD card and go.

Everything in one conversation

🔎

Finds free models

Searches Thingiverse, Printables & MakerWorld at once — thumbnails, creators, licenses.

🧠

Reasons about the print

Picks layer height, infill, walls, supports & brim from the model’s geometry and your goal.

📐

Inspects real geometry

Reads true dimensions, volume, triangle count & watertightness before it commits.

🧩

Multi-part & multi-plate

Auto-arranges parts, splits across plates when they don’t fit, and slices each one.

📤

Your own CAD too

Drag in STL · 3MF · OBJ · AMF · STEP — or a ZIP of parts — and it flows straight to slicing.

🍰

Real numbers

Estimated print time, filament in grams & metres, cost, and layer count — every slice.

Ready when you are

Slicely runs on macOS and drives your local PrusaSlicer. Searching & importing work without it; inspecting & slicing need it installed.

Bring your own Claude API key · your key stays on your Mac.
